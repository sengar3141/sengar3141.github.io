My Home Office

There will be always a need of a personal space which user can customise according to his/her needs. People need seclusion and less distraction from the surrounding so that they can focus on their work.

While researching for the project we realised that because of staying together with the parents and family, even after marraige the dynamics of Indian house holds are very different from the expected work from home culture. The space available at home is also very less in urban settlement for crating a room or home office. Even in the case of coworking environment there is lack of personal space for the work related to graphics and other jobs related to creative fields. So, after ideation for 3 weeks and discussing and weighting all the possibility we came around the following design brief.
We need to design a furniture which allows personal space and scope of customisation for the user.

I sketched more then 50 concept for the object then after doing SWOT analysis end-up with the following ideas We created form models and full scale ergonomic mockups to test the comfort and feasibility to the furniture.
We also added the possibilities of making the furniture such that, it can also be distributed to the company employees those prefer to work from home and it can also be used to provide personal space in co-working environment.

The design is flat pack easy to assemble and customisable according the usage needs of the users.

The work of designing the small add on products like speaker, lights, penholder and etc. are in progress
